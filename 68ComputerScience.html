68ComputerScience

To quote from Boston University, Department of Computer Science, "What is Computer Science?": "Computer Science is the systematic study of the feasibility, structure, expression, and mechanization of the methodical processes (or algorithms) that underlie the acquisition, representation, processing, storage, communication of, and access to information, whether such information is encoded as bits and bytes in a computer memory or transcribed in genes and protein structures in a biological cell."
