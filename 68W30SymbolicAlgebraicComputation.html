68W30 Symbolic computation and algebraic computation

This map presents the organization of the references and resources for symbolic and algebraic computation, and it is shared by topics 68N15 (Programming languages), and 68W32 (Algorithms on strings). It also contains links to 68W27 (Online algorithms).

The codes "68N15", "68W30"... correspond to the MSC2010 classification. For an introduction to the MSC2010, see "A Gentle Introduction to the Mathematics Subject Classification Scheme", at The Mathematical Atlas web site (developed at the Department of Mathematical Sciences , Northern Illinois University).

Lookup at sites ordered according to MSC2010

 Search arXiv.org

arXiv.org is the prime site for preprints, where physicists, mathematicians, computer scientists, quantitative biologists, and researchers from many other fields publicly share their findings before they are published in peer-reviewed journals. To get a taste for the use of MSC2010 codes, go to the arXiv math site, click on the drop-down list arrow at the right of "Author(s)", select "ACM/MSC Class", type "68N15" or, if you prefer, "68W30", in the next (blank) field, and leave the rest of the dialog as it is. Click on "Do Search" to see some of the latest and greatest research preprints on these topics.
