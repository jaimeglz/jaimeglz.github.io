 Get a taste of the power of MSC2010

  Search arXiv.org

arXiv.org is the prime site for preprints, where physicists, mathematicians, computer scientists and quantitative biologists publicly share their findings before these are published in peer-reviewed journals. To get a taste for the use of MSC2010 codes, go to the arXiv math site, click on the drop-down list arrow at the right of "Author(s)", select "ACM/MSC Class", type "01A55" in the next (blank) field, and leave the rest of the dialog as it is. Click on "Do Search" to see some of the latest and greatest research preprints on 01A45 (History of mathematics and mathematicians, Seventeenth Century).
